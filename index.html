I am a Senior Data Scientist in the MOH Office for Healthcare Transformation, under the Ministry of Health in Singapore, where I lead the Digital Health Applications Team. We drive initiatives that transform care delivery and health promotion with tech innovation across community, primary, and tertiary care settings.

I am also Adjunct Senior Lecturer in the Department of Statistics and Data Science at the National University of Singapore (NUS), where I lecture on applied statistical inference and machine learning. Finally, I am also a member of the Systems Biology and Chromatin Computation Lab at NUS, where I work in epigenomics -- as a masters student, I am training in molecular biology, genetic engineering, and broader topics in biotechnology.

Previously, I was a Vice President & Executive Director at Goldman Sachs in Hong Kong, where I helped to establish a machine learning team in the Securities division.
